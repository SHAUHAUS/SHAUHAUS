PROJECT 01 / LALA + LOUISE / "GREETINGS FROM LALA LAND"

This project has two primary goals:

1. To engage in a detailed and advanced process of poster design, one that goes beyond that of the realm of a flyer, or an advertisement, but exists as a symbol of our understanding and utilization of advanced design methods, and reflects its intended message in an appropriate, exciting, and innovative way. A poster is an image enhanced, and a foundational medium for the entire practice of design and communication.

2. To work together (and independently) to collectively tell the (continued) story of Charles LaLa Evans and Louise Evans. This project is in honor of LaLa and his continued contribution to making Starkville a more alive and welcoming place, and in remembrance of Louise.
